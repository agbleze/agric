Agrimetry is as an organization employs geospatial technology to support precision agriculture for both commercial and non-commercial purposes. We analyze earth observation data into readily availabe information products that are required to make well-informed decision on farm resource management, crop farming among others.
Our focus also includes disseminating available information such as meteorological information to farmers and allowing uptake of farmers concern to agric extension officers. Our vision is clear

Our clients are segmented into two main target markets as follows

  • Small scale farmers
  • Commercial farmers
  • Large scale Agribusiness firms
We will acquire satellite images
We will process them into various information products
We will store the information on a cloud platform
Framers and all clients will use apps to download or view the products of their choice
The system provides various automation options as follows;
	Farmer walks around his field and his app acquires the GPS coordinates of the farmland
	 >> Then farmer specifies the information product needed and app synchronizes with cloud database and use GPS coordinates to clip information for his farm
	  >>>Farmer can predefine the coordinate of farmland and information products required and frequency for view that information and the automatically delivers it without farmer having to be on the field or acting

Glossary

Precision farming
It entails the use of geospatial technology to enable improved accuracy in farm resource management by gaining knowledge in the variation of farm resource spatially
GIS
Is an acronym for Geographic Information System which is an integrated computer enable system for capture, retrieval, analysis, visualization and display of spatially referneced data.
